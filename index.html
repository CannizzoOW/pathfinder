Pathfinders FAQ:

🗲 What can Pathfinder's provide help with?

Live VOD Review, VOD Review, Gameplay fundamentals, Team strategy and Communication improvement

🗲 Where can I get support for different types of issues?

Moderation or Community issues: Contact the moderators in the official Marvel Rivals Discord.

Technical or Account issues: Use the in-game customer support button or the official Marvel Rivals support page.

🗲 How much does it cost?

Free of charge.

🗲 I just started playing, can someone help?

Pathfinder's specialize in providing feedback and advice for gameplay more advanced than the introductory hero basics. Finding a video guide online will be more beneficial to learn the fundamentals of a hero and once you feel you are struggling with specific gameplay mechanics, rather than the hero's kit it will be easier for a Pathfinder to provide guidance.

If you haven't played the hero yet, you should watch a video guide

🗲 How do I improve my aim?

Aim improvement is mostly about practice and repetition. Although Pathfinders don't provide dedicated aim training, you can work on it through the in-game training range, consistent play over time or use dedicated programs to improve your aim.
